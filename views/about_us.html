Our Story

Crafting elegance since 1985

Our Heritage

Founded in 1985 by master jeweler Isabella Laurent, Laurent Jewelers has been creating exquisite fine jewelry for over three decades. What began as a small workshop in the heart of the city has grown into a renowned name in the world of luxury jewelry.

Our philosophy is simple: we believe that every piece of jewelry tells a story. From engagement rings that symbolize a lifetime commitment to statement pieces that celebrate personal milestones, our creations are designed to capture moments and emotions that last forever.

With an unwavering commitment to craftsmanship and an eye for timeless design, we have established ourselves as purveyors of exceptional jewelry that bridges the gap between traditional artistry and contemporary elegance.

Our Journey

1985

A Vision Takes Shape

Isabella Laurent establishes her first boutique workshop, creating custom pieces for a select clientele.

1997

Expanding Horizons

The opening of our flagship store marks the beginning of a new era, bringing our designs to a wider audience.

2008

Innovation in Design

Launch of our signature collection, blending traditional craftsmanship with contemporary aesthetic sensibilities.

2023

A Digital Transformation

Our online boutique opens, allowing us to share our passion for fine jewelry with customers worldwide.

Our Craftsmanship

At Laurent Jewelers, we combine time-honored techniques with modern innovation. Each piece passes through the hands of our skilled artisans, who have honed their craft through years of dedication and expertise.

We source only the finest materials – ethically mined diamonds and gemstones, precious metals of the highest quality, and rare materials that meet our exacting standards. Our commitment to excellence means that no detail is too small, no element overlooked in the pursuit of perfection.

From initial sketch to final polish, every step in our creation process is guided by a passion for beauty and an unwavering attention to detail. The result is jewelry that doesn't just adorn – it elevates, celebrates, and becomes a cherished part of your personal story.
